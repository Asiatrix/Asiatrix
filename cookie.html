Cookie Policy

Understand how we use cookies and your choices regarding them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help websites function and provide better user experiences. They can store user preferences, session details, and analytics information.

Why We Use Cookies

At Asiatrix, we use cookies to enhance your experience, provide personalized content, analyze site usage, and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to function properly.
Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site.
Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
Marketing Cookies: Used to show relevant ads and content.

Your Choices

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Updates

This cookie policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to review it regularly.
